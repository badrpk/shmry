# SHMRY Commercial Offer

## Positioning

SHMRY is a lightweight infrastructure intelligence platform that unifies telemetry, governance, billing, topology, and automation into a single operational control plane.

## Main Commercial Offers

### Infrastructure Intelligence
- Monitoring
- Runtime telemetry
- Regional visibility
- Operational analytics

### Governance & Audit
- Git-backed operational checkpoints
- Policy-governed automation
- Structured audit history

### Managed SHMRY
- Deployment
- Hosting
- Support
- Integration
- Custom automation

## Target Customers

- SMBs
- Telecoms
- Universities
- Datacenters
- Governments
- Industrial clusters
