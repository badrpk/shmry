# SHMRY: Uraan 5Es Strategic Positioning

SHMRY provides the digital backbone for the CPEC 2.0 Industrial Clusters through:

1. **Exports:** Streamlining telemetry for export-oriented SEZs.
2. **E-Pakistan:** Sovereign cloud governance for localized data.
3. **Environment:** Green Growth monitoring for industrial efficiency.
4. **Energy:** Telemetry for regional power-grid integration.
5. **Equity:** Distributed infrastructure across the Islamabad-Lahore-Karachi triangle.
